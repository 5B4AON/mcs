{{ expanded ? '▼' : '▶' }} Screen Wake Lock
@if (expanded) {
Prevents the device screen from locking due to inactivity. On mobile devices, screen lock suspends network connectivity which interrupts Firebase RTDB relay and other live features.

When enabled, the app requests a screen wake lock to keep the display active while the page is in the foreground. The lock is automatically released when you switch to another app or tab, and re-acquired when you return.
