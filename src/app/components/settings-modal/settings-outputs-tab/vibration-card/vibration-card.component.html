Haptic vibration feedback while keying, mirroring the sidetone output. Works for both TX and RX key events from any input source.

Android only. The Vibration API is supported in most Android browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge). iOS Safari and most desktop browsers do not support haptic vibration.
Compensates for the physical spin-up delay of vibration motors on Android devices. Fires a short pre-pulse when the key goes down to prime the motor, and extends vibration slightly past key-up so short elements (dits) are more perceptible. Disable this if vibration feels out of sync or you prefer the standard behaviour.
