Decoded characters from any input are played back as individual morse elements (dits and dahs) at Encoder WPM speed. Ideal for an Arduino Pro Micro with MIDIUSB library — the Arduino's digital pins can be wired to keying circuits.

Straight Key note fires during every element (dit or dah). Dit Paddle fires during dits only. Dah Paddle fires during dahs only. During each element, both the Straight Key and the element-specific note fire simultaneously.
